Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer?

I specialize in full-stack web development using React.js, Django and WordPress My services include responsive website design, web application development, e-commerce solutions, API development, and website maintenance.

How long does it take to complete a website?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity and scope. A simple website can be completed in 2-4 weeks, while more complex applications might take 2-3 months. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

What is your development process?

My development process includes: 1) Requirements gathering and planning, 2) Design and prototyping, 3) Development and coding, 4) Testing and quality assurance, 5) Deployment, and 6) Maintenance and support. I keep clients updated throughout each phase.

Do you provide website maintenance?

Yes, I offer website maintenance services to ensure your site remains secure, up-to-date, and optimized. This includes regular updates, security patches, performance optimization, and content updates upon request.

How do we get started on a project?

To get started, simply reach out to me via the contact form, email, or WhatsApp. I'll schedule an initial consultation where we'll discuss your needs, goals, timeline, and budget. After that, I'll send you a detailed proposal and a pricing quote.

Do you work with clients internationally?

Yes, I work with clients worldwide. With modern communication tools like email, WhatsApp, Zoom, and project management systems, we can collaborate effectively regardless of your location or time zone.
